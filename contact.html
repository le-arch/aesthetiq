Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I get started with a design project?

Begin by scheduling a consultation through our contact form or by calling our studio. We'll discuss your vision, budget, and timeline before creating a customized proposal.

What is your design process?

Our process includes discovery consultation, concept development, design refinement, implementation, and final reveal. We guide you through each step to ensure your vision is realized.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, we offer virtual design services for clients outside our local area. We use video calls, digital presentations, and detailed documentation to manage remote projects effectively.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope, but most residential projects take 3-6 months from concept to completion. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

How do you handle project budgets?

We work within your specified budget and provide transparent cost estimates. Our fees are structured based on project scope, with options for hourly rates or flat project fees.

Do you offer sustainable design options?

Absolutely. We prioritize eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient solutions, and sustainable practices. We can help you achieve LEED certification or other sustainability goals.
