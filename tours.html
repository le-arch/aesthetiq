How to Setup VR Experience

1

Choose Your Device

Our VR tours work with:

  • VR Headsets (Oculus, HTC Vive)
  • Cardboard VR viewers
  • Desktop computers (mouse drag)
  • Mobile devices (touch swipe)
2

Prepare Your Equipment

For best experience:

  • Use headphones for spatial audio
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Enable fullscreen mode
  • Allow autoplay in browser settings
3

Start Your Tour

Once you're ready:

  • Click the VR button in any tour
  • Use arrow keys or swipe to navigate
  • Enable audio guide for narration
  • Click hotspots for room details
