About Design Aesthetiq

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Leonie Basil, Design Aesthetiq began as a small interior design studio with a big vision: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary homes. What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a team of 12 talented designers, each bringing unique perspectives and expertise to every project.

Over the past decade, we've completed over 300 projects across the country, from cozy apartments to luxury estates. Our work has been featured in prestigious design publications and has won several industry awards.
